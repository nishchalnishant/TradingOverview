# Section 4 — Chart Patterns

Chart patterns are **multi-candle (or multi-bar) structures** that reflect shifts in supply and demand. **Reversal patterns** suggest a change of trend; **continuation patterns** suggest a pause before the trend resumes. Breakouts, volume, and false breakouts are key to trading them.

## Pattern Types

- **Reversal:** Head and Shoulders, Double Top/Bottom, Triple Top/Bottom, Rounded Top/Bottom. Often after an extended move.
- **Continuation:** Bull/Bear Flag, Pennant, Ascending/Descending/Symmetrical Triangle, Rectangle, Cup and Handle. Pause within a trend.

---

## Trading Psychology

- **Breakouts:** Many fail (false breakouts); wait for **close** beyond the pattern and/or **volume** confirmation before committing.
- **Targets:** Use **measured moves** (e.g. H&S neckline to head = target below neckline) but always use a **stop** (e.g. beyond the pattern).
