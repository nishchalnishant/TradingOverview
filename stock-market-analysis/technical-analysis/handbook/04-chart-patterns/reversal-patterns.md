# Reversal Chart Patterns

Reversal patterns form after an **extended move** and suggest a **change of trend**. They are best traded on **breakout** of the pattern (e.g. neckline) with confirmation.

---

## Head and Shoulders (Bearish)

- **Shape:** Three peaks: **Left Shoulder**, **Head** (highest), **Right Shoulder**. The head is higher than both shoulders. Lows between them form a **neckline** (support). When price breaks **below the neckline**, the pattern is confirmed.
- **Psychology:** First peak (LS), stronger peak (H), then a weaker peak (RS) that fails to make a new high = buyers losing strength. Break of neckline = sellers in control.

```
        ●  Right Shoulder
       / \
      /   \    ●  Head
     /     \  / \
    ●       \/   \___
   / \   Left        \___ Neckline
  /   \  Shoulder         \
```

- **Measured move:** Distance from **head** to **neckline** (vertical). Project that distance **down** from the neckline break for a **target**.
- **Entry:** Short on **close below** neckline (or retest of neckline as resistance). **Stop:** Above right shoulder (or head).
- **Volume:** Ideally volume is higher on the left shoulder, lower on the right shoulder, and **increases** on the breakdown.

---

## Inverse Head and Shoulders (Bullish)

- **Shape:** Three **troughs**: Left Shoulder, **Head** (lowest), Right Shoulder. Neckline connects the highs between them. **Break above neckline** confirms.
- **Psychology:** Selling exhaustion at the head; buyers step in. Break above neckline = buyers in control.
- **Measured move:** Head to neckline distance; project **up** from neckline break.
- **Entry:** Long on close above neckline or retest. **Stop:** Below right shoulder or head.
- **Volume:** Often higher volume on the breakout above the neckline.

---

## Double Top

- **Shape:** Price makes **two similar highs** (resistance), pulls back, then fails to break above again. **Break below the swing low** between the two tops (the “neckline”) confirms.
- **Psychology:** Sellers defend the level twice; break below the low = trend turning bearish.
- **Target:** Approximate distance from highs to neckline, projected **down** from neckline break.

```
  ●       ●   Two tops
   \     /
    \   /
     \ /
      ●  Neckline (break below)
```

- **Entry:** Short on close below neckline. **Stop:** Above the double top highs.

---

## Double Bottom

- **Shape:** Two **similar lows** (support), then rally. **Break above the swing high** between the two bottoms confirms.
- **Psychology:** Buyers defend the level twice; break above = bullish reversal.
- **Target:** Distance from lows to neckline, projected **up** from break.
- **Entry:** Long on close above neckline. **Stop:** Below the double bottom lows.

---

## Triple Top / Triple Bottom

- **Shape:** Same idea as double top/bottom but with **three** tests of the level. Often shows stronger conviction (or more exhaustion) at the level.
- **Trading:** Break of the “neckline” (the swing low for triple top, swing high for triple bottom) with volume. Measured move and stop placement follow the same logic.

---

## Rounded Top (Dome)

- **Shape:** Price gradually **curves up** then **curves down**, forming a dome. No sharp V or double top—a slow rollover.
- **Psychology:** Gradual distribution; buyers are slowly replaced by sellers.
- **Entry:** Short on break below the rounded support (the base of the dome). **Stop:** Above the dome high.

---

## Rounded Bottom (Saucer / Bowl)

- **Shape:** Price **curves down** then **curves up**, forming a saucer. Gradual accumulation.
- **Psychology:** Sellers exhausted; buyers slowly take control.
- **Entry:** Long on break above the rounded resistance (rim of the saucer). **Stop:** Below the saucer low.

---

## Summary

| Pattern | Break level | Bias | Stop |
|---------|-------------|------|------|
| Head and Shoulders | Below neckline | Bearish | Above RS or head |
| Inverse H&S | Above neckline | Bullish | Below RS or head |
| Double Top | Below neckline | Bearish | Above tops |
| Double Bottom | Above neckline | Bullish | Below bottoms |
| Triple Top/Bottom | Beyond neckline | Reversal | Beyond pattern |
| Rounded Top | Below base | Bearish | Above dome |
| Rounded Bottom | Above rim | Bullish | Below saucer |
