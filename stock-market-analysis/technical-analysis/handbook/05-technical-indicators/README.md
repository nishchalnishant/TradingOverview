# Section 5 — Technical Indicators

Technical indicators are **mathematical calculations** on price and/or volume. They help with **trend**, **momentum**, **volatility**, and **volume** but should be used with **price action** and **structure**, not in isolation.

---

## General Principles

- **Lagging:** Most indicators are **lagging** (based on past price). They confirm; they don’t lead.
- **Context:** Same reading (e.g. RSI 70) can mean “overbought” in a range or “strong trend” in an uptrend. Always consider **trend** and **level**.
- **Overload:** Using too many indicators causes conflict and confusion. Pick a few that fit your strategy and timeframe.
