# Trend and Structure (HH/HL, LH/LL, Break of Structure)

## Concept

**Trend** is the prevailing direction of price. **Structure** is the sequence of swing highs and swing lows. In an **uptrend** we see **Higher Highs (HH)** and **Higher Lows (HL)**. In a **downtrend** we see **Lower Highs (LH)** and **Lower Lows (LL)**. A **break of structure (BOS)** is when price breaks a prior HH or LL.

---

## Uptrend Structure

```
      HH3
        ●
       / \
      /   \  HH2
     /     ●
    /     / \
   /     /   \  HH1
  ●     ●     ●
   \   / \   /
    \ /   \ /
     ●     ●
     HL1   HL2
```

- **Definition:** Each significant **low** is above the previous low (HL), and each significant **high** is above the previous high (HH).
- **Trading:** Look for longs on pullbacks to structure (e.g. prior HL or trendline); stop below the last HL.

---

## Downtrend Structure

```
  LH1   LH2
   ●     ●
    \   / \   /
     \ /   \ /
      ●     ●
       \   / \   /
        \ /   \ ●  LL1
         ●     \
                ●  LL2
```

- **Definition:** Each significant **high** is below the previous high (LH), and each significant **low** is below the previous low (LL).
- **Trading:** Look for shorts on rallies to structure; stop above the last LH.

---

## Break of Structure (BOS)

- **Bullish BOS:** Price breaks **above** a prior HH → suggests uptrend continuation (or start of new uptrend).
- **Bearish BOS:** Price breaks **below** a prior LL → suggests downtrend continuation (or start of new downtrend).
- **Context:** BOS after a pullback in a trend is often used as **entry confirmation** (e.g. long on break of last HH after a HL).

---

## Chart Interpretation

- **Swing points:** Define them consistently (e.g. pivot where left and right bars have lower highs for a swing high). Timeframe matters—higher TF = fewer, stronger swings.
- **BOS:** Confirm with **close** beyond the level; wicks alone can be stop hunts.

---

## Trading Psychology

- **Hindsight:** Structure is clear in hindsight; in real time, wait for **clear** HH/HL or LH/LL before assuming trend.
- **False breaks:** Not every BOS leads to a big move; use **targets** and **stops** so one failed BOS doesn’t hurt the account.

---

## Risk Management

- **Stop:** In an uptrend, stop below the last **HL** (or below a key support); in a downtrend, stop above the last **LH**.
- **Position size:** Same as elsewhere—risk per trade (e.g. 1%) = (entry − stop) × size.
