# Technical Analysis & Trading Handbook

A complete, structured knowledge base for learning trading—from beginner to advanced. This handbook covers market structure, price action, indicators, patterns, strategies, risk management, and psychology.

---

## How to Use This Handbook

- **Beginners:** Start with [01 - Market Basics](01-market-basics/README.md), then [02 - Price Action](02-price-action/README.md) and [03 - Candlestick Analysis](03-candlestick-analysis/README.md).
- **Intermediate:** Focus on [04 - Chart Patterns](04-chart-patterns/README.md), [05 - Technical Indicators](05-technical-indicators/README.md), and [07 - Trading Strategies](07-trading-strategies/README.md).
- **Advanced:** Study [06 - Advanced Concepts](06-advanced-concepts/README.md), [08 - Risk Management](08-risk-management/README.md), and [11 - Algorithmic Trading](11-algorithmic-trading/README.md).
- **Quick revision:** Use the [Revision folder](../../../Revision/) for cheat sheets and exam-style summaries.
