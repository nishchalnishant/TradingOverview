# Section 7 — Trading Strategies

This section documents **discrete strategies** by style: trend following, breakout, mean reversion, scalping, swing, and position trading. Each strategy includes setup conditions, entry/exit, stop placement, risk:reward, and backtesting suggestions.

| [Breakout](breakout-strategies.md) | Trade breakouts of range, levels, or patterns |

| [Scalping](scalping-strategies.md) | Short-term, small targets, quick exits |
| [Swing Trading](swing-trading.md) | Multi-day holds; structure and key levels |
| [Position Trading](position-trading.md) | Longer-term; trend and macro alignment |

---

## Choosing a Strategy

- **Timeframe:** Scalp = minutes; swing = days; position = weeks/months.
- **Personality:** Trend following requires patience; mean reversion requires discipline to fade; scalping requires focus and speed.
- **Market:** Trend strategies work in trending markets; mean reversion in ranges. Match strategy to **current** market regime when possible.
