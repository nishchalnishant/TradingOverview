# Three Candlestick Patterns

Three-candle patterns add a **middle** candle (often small or opposite to the trend) to show **indecision or transition** before a reversal. They tend to be stronger when they appear at key levels.

---

## Morning Star (Bullish)

- **Shape:**
  1. First: **Large bearish** candle (downtrend).
  2. Second: **Small body** (can be doji or small bullish/bearish), gaps down or near first close. Shows indecision.
  3. Third: **Large bullish** candle that closes well **into** the first candle’s body (ideally above its midpoint).
- **Meaning:** Selling (1), indecision (2), then strong buying (3). **Bullish** reversal.
- **Context:** At support or after a sustained decline.

```
  1 (bearish)    2 (small)    3 (bullish)
     ███            —           █████
     ███            █           █████
     ███                         █████
```

- **Entry:** Long on break above the high of the third candle. **Stop:** Below the low of candle 2 or 3.

---

## Evening Star (Bearish)

- **Shape:**
  1. First: **Large bullish** candle (uptrend).
  2. Second: **Small body** (doji or small candle), often gaps up. Indecision.
  3. Third: **Large bearish** candle that closes well **into** the first candle’s body.
- **Meaning:** Buying (1), indecision (2), then strong selling (3). **Bearish** reversal.
- **Context:** At resistance or after a sustained rally.

- **Entry:** Short on break below the low of the third candle. **Stop:** Above the high of candle 2 or 3.

---

## Three White Soldiers

- **Shape:** Three **consecutive bullish** candles. Each opens within the prior candle’s body and closes **higher** (ideally at or near its high). Progressive strength.
- **Meaning:** Strong, sustained buying. **Bullish** continuation or reversal (if after a decline).
- **Context:** Start of uptrend or after a pullback. Weaker if wicks appear on the third (exhaustion).

```
     ███
     ███  ███
     ███  ███  ███
```

- **Entry:** Long on pullback to the pattern or on break above the third candle high. **Stop:** Below the low of the first soldier.

---

## Three Black Crows

- **Shape:** Three **consecutive bearish** candles. Each opens within the prior candle’s body and closes **lower**. Progressive selling.
- **Meaning:** Strong, sustained selling. **Bearish** continuation or reversal (if after a rally).
- **Context:** In a downtrend or after a top. Reversal signal when at support.

- **Entry:** Short on bounce to the pattern or on break below the third candle low. **Stop:** Above the high of the first crow.

---

## Inside Bar

- **Shape:** A candle whose **high is below** the prior candle’s high and whose **low is above** the prior candle’s low. The current candle is “inside” the previous one.
- **Meaning:** **Consolidation** or **coil**; often precedes a breakout. Direction of breakout is the signal.
- **Context:** After a move or at a level; breakout can be continuation or reversal.

```
  Mother bar        Inside bar
  ─────●─────       ────●────
  │   ███   │       │  ███  │
  │   ███   │       │  ███  │
  ─────●─────       ────●────
```

- **Entry:** Long on break above mother high; short on break below mother low. **Stop:** Opposite side of mother (or inside bar).

---

## Outside Bar (Engulfing Bar)

- **Shape:** A candle whose **high is above** the prior high and **low is below** the prior low. The current candle “engulfs” the previous range.
- **Meaning:** Expansion of range; strong momentum in the **direction of the close**. If close is near high → bullish; if close is near low → bearish.
- **Context:** At a level, can signal breakout or reversal depending on close and next candle.

- **Entry:** In direction of the outside bar’s close, with confirmation. **Stop:** Opposite extreme of the outside bar.
