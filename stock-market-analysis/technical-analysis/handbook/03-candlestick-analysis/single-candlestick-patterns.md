# Single Candlestick Patterns

Single-candle patterns are formed by **one** candle. Interpretation depends on **where** they appear (after a rally, at support, etc.).

---

## Doji

- **Shape:** Open ≈ close; very small body. Upper and/or lower wicks can be long.
- **Meaning:** Indecision; balance between buyers and sellers. After a strong move, can signal **exhaustion** or potential reversal.
- **Context:** More significant at support (bullish) or resistance (bearish), or after a long trend.

```
    —  (small body, wicks vary)
```

- **Trading:** Don’t trade doji alone; wait for **next-candle confirmation** (e.g. bullish candle after doji at support).

---

## Hammer

- **Shape:** Small body at the **top** of the range; **long lower wick**; little or no upper wick. Can be green or red.
- **Meaning:** Sellers pushed price down; buyers recovered strongly. **Bullish** reversal signal when it appears after a decline or at support.
- **Context:** Best at support, at a HL in an uptrend, or after a down move.

```
    │
    █
    █
    │
    │
    │  (long lower wick)
```

- **Entry:** Long on break above hammer high or next bullish candle. **Stop:** Below hammer low.

---

## Hanging Man

- **Shape:** Same as hammer (small body at top, long lower wick) but appears **after an uptrend** or near resistance.
- **Meaning:** Buyers lifted price, then sellers sold off; the long lower wick shows weakness. **Bearish** warning.
- **Context:** At resistance or after a rally; confirm with next bearish candle.

- **Entry:** Short on break below hanging man low or next bearish candle. **Stop:** Above hanging man high.

---

## Inverted Hammer

- **Shape:** Small body at the **bottom** of the range; **long upper wick**; little or no lower wick.
- **Meaning:** Buyers tried to push price up; sellers rejected. At support after a decline, can be a **bullish** reversal signal (buyers testing).
- **Context:** After a downtrend or at support; confirmation on next candle.

```
    │
    │
    │  (long upper wick)
    █
    █
```

- **Entry:** Long on break above inverted hammer high with confirmation. **Stop:** Below hammer low.

---

## Shooting Star

- **Shape:** Small body at the **bottom** of the range; **long upper wick**; little or no lower wick. Same shape as inverted hammer but **after an uptrend**.
- **Meaning:** Buyers pushed price up; sellers rejected strongly. **Bearish** reversal signal.
- **Context:** At resistance or after a rally.

- **Entry:** Short on break below shooting star low or next bearish candle. **Stop:** Above shooting star high.

---

## Marubozu

- **Shape:** **No** or very small wicks; body spans (or nearly spans) the full range from low to high.
- **Bullish Marubozu:** Open = low, close = high. Strong buying throughout the period.
- **Bearish Marubozu:** Open = high, close = low. Strong selling throughout the period.
- **Meaning:** Strong conviction in the direction of the body. In a trend, can indicate **continuation**; at a key level, can indicate breakout or rejection.

```
Bullish:  ████   Bearish:  ████
          ████              ████
          ████              ████
          ████              ████
```

- **Trading:** Use as confirmation of momentum; entry in direction of body at structure or on breakout.

## Summary Table

| Pattern | Location | Bias | Stop |
|---------|----------|------|------|
| Doji | Any (best at S/R) | Neutral / reversal if confirmed | Beyond pattern |
| Hammer | After decline, support | Bullish | Below hammer low |
| Hanging Man | After rally, resistance | Bearish | Above high |
| Inverted Hammer | After decline, support | Bullish | Below low |
| Shooting Star | After rally, resistance | Bearish | Above high |
| Marubozu | Trend or breakout | Direction of body | Beyond pattern |
