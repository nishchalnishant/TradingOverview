# Trading Setups Checklist

Step-by-step checklists for **pre-trade**, **trade execution**, and **post-trade review**. Use every time to stay consistent.

---

## Pre-Trade Checklist

**Before the session / before looking for trades:**

- [ ] **Market regime:** Is the market **trending** or **ranging** on my timeframe? (HH/HL or LH/LL vs horizontal S/R.)
- [ ] **Higher timeframe bias:** What is the trend on the next higher TF? (Only take longs in uptrend, shorts in downtrend—or note “counter-trend” if I allow it.)
- [ ] **Key levels:** Where are support and resistance? (Mark them.)
- [ ] **Session / news:** Am I trading in a good session (liquidity)? Any major news soon? (Avoid or reduce size.)
- [ ] **Mental state:** Am I calm? Not revenge-trading from last loss? Not overtrading from FOMO? (If no, do not trade.)
- [ ] **Risk for today:** What is my max risk today (e.g. 2% total)? How many trades max (e.g. 3–5)?

**Before each specific trade:**

- [ ] **Setup matches my plan:** Does this trade fit my **written** setup (e.g. pullback to EMA in uptrend, bullish candle)?
- [ ] **Context:** Is there a **level** (support/resistance) and/or **structure** (HL, HH)? Am I not trading in the middle of nowhere?
- [ ] **Entry:** Do I have a clear **entry** (e.g. break of candle high, or limit at level)?
- [ ] **Stop:** Where is my **stop**? (Below/above structure or level.) Is it **before** I enter?
- [ ] **Target:** Where is my **target**? (Next structure, or fixed R:R e.g. 1:2.)
- [ ] **Position size:** Size = (Account × Risk%) / |Entry − Stop|. Did I calculate it?
- [ ] **R:R:** Is risk:reward at least 1:1.5 or 1:2? If not, skip or adjust target/stop.

---

## Trade Execution Checklist

**When entering:**

- [ ] Enter only when **all** setup conditions are met. No “close enough.”
- [ ] Use the **order type** I decided (limit at level vs market on break).
- [ ] **Place stop order** immediately after entry (or have it attached).
- [ ] **Do not** move stop against the trade. (Only to breakeven or to lock profit if that’s my rule.)

**While in the trade:**

- [ ] No **adding to losers** (unless predefined scale-in plan).
- [ ] If target is hit, **exit** (or take partial and trail the rest per plan).
- [ ] If stop is hit, **accept** it. No revenge trade.

---

## Post-Trade Review Checklist

**Right after the trade (or end of day):**

- [ ] **Log the trade:** Instrument, timeframe, setup type, entry, exit, stop, target, P&L ($ and R), date/time.
- [ ] **Rule adherence:** Did I follow my plan? (Yes/No.) If no, what did I do and why?
- [ ] **Emotional state:** What was I feeling? (Calm, fearful, greedy, revenge, FOMO.)
- [ ] **Screenshot (optional):** Chart at entry for later review.

**Weekly review:**

- [ ] **Win rate** this week. **Average R** (or avg win vs avg loss).
- [ ] **Best trade** and **worst trade**—what went right/wrong?
- [ ] **Rule breaks:** How many times did I not follow my plan? What triggered it?
- [ ] **Adjustments:** Any change to my plan? (Only with data and calm thought; not emotional.)

**Monthly review:**

- [ ] **Expectancy** (avg R per trade). **Max drawdown.** **Profit factor.**
- [ ] **By setup type:** Which setups (e.g. pullback vs breakout) performed best?
- [ ] **Plan update:** Do I need to refine entry/exit/stop or risk? Document changes.

---

## One-Line Reminders

- **Pre:** Trend + level + setup + stop + size + R:R. Only then enter.
- **During:** Stop is sacred. No add to loser. No move stop against.
- **After:** Log every trade. Weekly: win rate, rule breaks. Monthly: expectancy, drawdown, plan.
